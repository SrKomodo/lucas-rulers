Genaille-Lucas Rulers are special rulers designed to quickly do multiplication without having to do the math

How does it work?

Let's say you want to multiply 618 by 7

First you put the Index, 6, 1 and 8 rulers on the table

Then you pick the first number in the 7th row of the right-most ruler

Now all you have to do is to follow the triangles from ruler to ruler, and once you get to the Index just read all the numbers you passed through from left to right to get the result!
